List of research universities in the United States

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a list of research universities in the United States classified as Doctoral Universities in the Carnegie Classification of Institutions of Higher Education. Research institutions are a subset of doctoral degree-granting institutions and conduct research.[1]
